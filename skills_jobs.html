Skills & Jobs

Let us suppose that a group of venture capitalists hears about your project, and is so impressed that they wish to fund you to develop it further for say six months. You will be the manager of a team of 4 people to deliver the project outcomes. What position description would be appropriate? Write 4 position descriptions for people that you would employ to take your project to the next phase. You will need to consider what skills are appropriate, which may include specific technical expertise, team work experience, leadership and management techniques, and innovative thinking.

Accountant


About the Role


As a startup, we expect most of our funds to come from owner’s equity and funding from other interested parties. Furthermore, every member in our team has limited knowledge in finance and budget planning. In addition, since we are assuming we are receiving funds from a Venture Capitalist, we as a group have decided it is best to hire an accountant with degrees related in commerce (or a similar field) to handle our financial operations within our business. This includes creating and maintaining relevant files such as balance sheets, cash flow sheets and income statements.

Key Responsibilites

  • Maintaining accurate, relevant and timely financial records such as balance sheets and income statements.
  • Prepare and distribute company reports- Finance, Sales and Management on weekly and monthly basis.
  • Assist with month end and year end processes, including balance sheet reconciliations, compliance, audits and reporting within stipulated deadlines.
  • Prepare and present operational forecast(s) for financial and operational reporting.
  • Manage stocktake of inventory to ensure accurate collection of date and provide reports on inventory status against plan.
  • Undertaking reviews of financial reports and data.
  • Strategise financial plans to reduce costs and maximise profits.

Skills & Experience

  • Advanced Excel skills..
  • The ability to build strong relationships internally and externally.
  • The ability to represent the Company in a professional manner.
  • Strong numeric competency.
  • Manage stocktake of inventory to ensure accurate collection of date and provide reports on inventory status against plan.
  • High attention to detail.

UX Designer


About the Role


EduTech is a newly created startup that aims to transform the world of early education through providing exciting new and innovative ways for children to be inspired and learn from. Our amazing team is actively looking for a talented UX designer to jump on board and work towards creating the ultimate user experience for EduTech’s users. You will be surrounded by a welcoming culture of optimism and integrity, as you and your peers strive towards an ever growing application with smiles and inspiration to make a difference.

Key Responsibilites

  • User research to find out the latest needs and expectations from EduTech users to ensure our app is up to date and reflects the changing views of society.
  • Usability and user experience testing, ensuring users are able to navigate the app and get input on whether certain features are useful and applicable.
  • Designing and prototyping newer features and update for the existing app.
  • Create wireframes for each new update.
  • Optimise existing UX to better the application.

Skills & Experience

  • Experience with the concepts of user experience, and principles applicable.
  • Ability to positively work within a small team with other members with the same goals.
  • Communicate with clients and other team members.
  • Ability to troubleshoot UX related problems.
  • Ability to conduct user testing .
  • Have a portfolio of previous designs.
