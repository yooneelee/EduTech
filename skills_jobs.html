This includes creating and maintaining relevant files such as balance sheets, cash flow sheets and income statements.

Key Responsibilites

  • Maintaining accurate, relevant and timely financial records such as balance sheets and income statements.
  • Prepare and distribute company reports- Finance, Sales and Management on weekly and monthly basis.
  • Assist with month end and year end processes, including balance sheet reconciliations, compliance, audits and reporting within stipulated deadlines.
  • Prepare and present operational forecast(s) for financial and operational reporting.
  • Manage stocktake of inventory to ensure accurate collection of date and provide reports on inventory status against plan.
  • Undertaking reviews of financial reports and data.
  • Strategise financial plans to reduce costs and maximise profits.
